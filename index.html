Duplication of S&P500 with Stock Prices

 Sat, 31/12/2016  —  Tomoaki Fujii

Economic indicators are statistics to measure growth and contraction of econmics. Trading based on such indicators, what is called technical trading, is one of the main trends in recent stock trading. Especially S&P500;(Standard and Poor's 500 Index) is frequently used as a bench mark to evaluate portfolio -- a grouping of financial assets like stocks. Roughly speaking, constructing a portfolio with better return and lower risk than S&P500; is one of the main goals for strock trading. Therefore, clarfiying the relation between S&P500; and stock prices are important to construct a good portfolio.
